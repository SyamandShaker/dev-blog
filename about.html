About

My career path...

My name is Syamand SHAKER, Kurdish and born in Ataba, Syria, aged 22, arrived in Switzerland in October 2016. Since then, I have actively participated in the social and associative life of the Canton of Fribourg.

Beforehand, I have been assiduously participating in integration courses (French, German, civics, mathematics, computer science and general culture) at the Professional and Artisanal School of Fribourg (EPAI). This allowed me to integrate perfectly into my new country and to speak French fluently.

My latest achievements: obtaining my B residence permit and obtaining my car license.

My goal...

I would like to be able to follow a training in the field of computer science (apprenticeship of computer scientist) because since my childhood, I have been passionate about this field; my family was the first in my village to have a computer at home.

I was immediately fascinated by "this machine" and how it works. Since then, my passion for this field has never ceased. My internships in the field of computer science in the Canton of Fribourg have convinced me that this field is for me. The feedback from the internship managers has been very positive and they encourage me to persevere in this direction and to make it my profession.

For this reason, I followed the Powercoders training from April to July 2020. Powercoders is a non-profit association and their training program is self-financed and free of charge for the selected participants.

I had the opportunity via POWERCODERS to follow a 13 weeks full time coding bootcamp. Today, I am actively looking for an internship that will prepare me to start an apprenticeship in computer science. I need professional practice!

I am a communicative, responsible, autonomous and very persevering person. Moreover, I am adaptable to all situations and I have great ease in integrating myself into a work team. I have the necessary computer skills because I am a person who is willing to work hard to achieve his goal.
